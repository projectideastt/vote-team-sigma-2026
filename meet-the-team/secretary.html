Who I Am

A Sevilla parent, medical professional, and committed servant-leader.

  • A Medical Doctor and Public Health Leader with over 15 years of experience in clinical medicine, health systems, policy, governance, and team leadership.
  • Has worked nationally with the Ministry of Health, regionally with CARPHA, and internationally with the CDC.
  • Professional background shaped by structure, fairness, confidentiality, and service.

What I Bring

Qualified leadership with integrity, discipline, and professionalism.

  • Strong administrative, governance, policy-development, and communication skills.
  • Experience leading multidisciplinary teams, coordinating programmes, and implementing evidence-based systems.
  • A focus on improving accountability, clarity, and outcomes.

Proven Results

Reliable service, real contribution, and quiet work that strengthened SPPS.

  • Served as Lead of the Operations Committee, coordinating key operational matters and supporting a more structured response to school needs.
  • Helped ensure issues were reviewed, discussed, and escalated appropriately so the Board could make informed decisions.
  • Led in the development of Board-approved operational policies to support clearer systems, stronger governance, and consistent decision-making.
  • Acted as Stand-In Secretary when required, supporting documentation, administrative duties, and continuity of Board function.
  • Served as a Subject Matter Expert during Parent/Teacher consultations, offering professional insight where further medical assessment or support may have been appropriate.
  • Worked to support child-centred, compassionate decisions guided by inclusiveness and equity.

Vision for the Next Year

Honest administration. Accountable governance. No child left behind.

  • Provide honest and accountable administrative support to the Board and SPPS.
  • Uphold confidentiality, professionalism, school policies, and by-laws.
  • Support the School Administration, Parent Body, and students wherever practical and needed.

My Commitment

Transparency and fit-for-purpose solutions for the SPPS community.

  • To support every child at SPPS in their educational journey and continue contributing as a subject matter expert where appropriate.
