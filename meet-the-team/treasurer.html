Who I Am

A Sevilla mother first, a qualified accountant, and an advocate for transparent governance.

  • For seven years, Sevilla has been a community where her three children grow and learn.
  • As a parent, she understands the desires and anxieties of the parent body because she lives them daily.
  • Her mission is to help build a school with secure and transparent systems that maximise benefits for SPPS children.

What I Bring

Over 15 years of financial expertise.

  • A Chartered Accountant with over 15 years of experience transforming financial and corporate structures across public and private sectors.
  • Specialises in taking broken accounting structures and transforming them into transparent financial systems.

Proven Results

Modernised fiscal management and due diligence.

  • Helped move Sevilla toward a surplus position and improved cash balances.
  • Streamlined cash-flow systems and supported safer handling and approval practices.
  • Supported targeted arrears settlement and channelled recovered funds into washroom and classroom renovations and school events.
  • Supported a five-year historical audit process to identify discrepancies, address gaps, and strengthen safeguards around school funds.

Vision for the Next Year

From stabilisation to growth, with continued financial accountability.

  • Continue the modernisation toward a fully cashless campus.
  • Strengthen the financial policy environment for sustainable growth across the school system.
  • Prioritise revenue generation toward a modernised SPPS, with targeted facility upgrades and stronger financial planning.

My Commitment

Transparency and fit-for-purpose solutions for the SPPS community.

  • To manage the books with professional skill, protect parent contributions with transparency, and serve with the heart of a mother who wants the best for the children of SPPS.
