Who I Am

A change leader with a passion for progress, growth, and goals realisation.

  • Over 20 years of executive management leadership experience globally.
  • Currently serves as Group Chief Executive Officer at Wilog-Group Holdings Limited, with offices in China, Trinidad and Tobago, and the USA.
  • Professional experience grounded in change leadership, organisational transformation, empathy, and care.

What I Bring

Executive leadership, organisational transformation, process re-engineering, and people-centred change management.

  • A focus on fixing broken systems through structure, process improvement, and strong leadership.
  • Experience leading people through change by understanding organisational culture and the core values of the people involved.
  • A practical, hands-on approach to rebuilding systems and restoring confidence.

Proven Results

Growth and transformation of SPPS through recovery, stabilisation, accountability, and renewed school pride.

  • Prioritised recovery and stabilisation of core internal systems after assuming leadership in 2025.
  • Supported the development and deployment of stronger internal controls with the Board team.
  • Commenced external audits to better understand historical issues and report findings in the spirit of transparency and accountability.
  • Helped move SPPS toward a cash surplus position and a more manageable debt-to-revenue position.
  • Supported the introduction of an accounting system to track receivables, payables, receipts, e-banking, admissions, and databases.
  • Supported improvements to curriculum alignment, teacher readiness, school facilities, daily operations, pick-up and drop-off arrangements, and student recreation.
  • Helped SPPS host major events on the school grounds, including Easter Extravaganza and Sports Day.
  • Supported corporate and private-sector engagement for possible classroom, technology, playground, auxiliary-service, and bursary support.

Vision for the Next Year

Forward, never backward.

  • Implement stronger quality management and measurement systems.
  • Improve business efficiency, revenue growth, savings, cost control, care management, and security.
  • Continue defining expectations, fixing broken processes, empowering where required, and helping students grow into future leaders, thinkers, and innovators.

My Commitment

Accountable leadership and loyalty in service with no compromises.

  • To continue serving with accountability, discipline, transparency, and a focus on the best interests of SPPS students and families.
