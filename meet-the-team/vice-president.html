Who I Am

A Sevilla parent, IT and digital innovation professional, and advocate for youth empowerment.

  • A multidisciplinary professional in IT, risk management, and corporate governance.
  • Holds a Bachelor of Science Degree (Hons) in Computer Information Systems accredited at London Metropolitan University.
  • Has spent approximately 25 years serving both the private and public sectors in Trinidad and Tobago.
  • Has served as IT Specialist with PSCU Credit Union Co-operative Society Limited and as Chairman of the PSCU Youth Advisory Committee.

What I Bring

A focus on empowering students through greater access to digital innovation.

  • A practical understanding of the needs of the SPPS community from service as Vice President.
  • Experience supporting innovation, school communication, digital systems, and community engagement.
  • A commitment to tools and systems that improve efficiency, accountability, and student readiness.

Proven Results

Retooling SPPS for growth, efficiency, empowerment, and stronger digital readiness.

  • Introduced and supported digital filing and records-management systems to improve efficiency, information access, and administrative accountability.
  • Supported the implementation of Google Classroom and digital learning capabilities to improve communication and student engagement.
  • Contributed to the growth of the school’s digital presence through social media, branding, and content creation.
  • Provided strategic guidance and support to the Finance Committee to strengthen oversight, budgeting, and accountability.
  • Promoted transparent and structured hiring processes aligned with institutional standards and values.
  • Supported KPI-driven performance evaluation to encourage staff development and institutional improvement.
  • Supported initiatives to align with Ministry of Education curriculum rubrics and educational standards.
  • Supported expanded extracurricular programmes including chess, karate, drama, music, and dance.

Vision for the Next Year

Building on gains toward a modern, technology-driven school community.

  • Develop and launch a comprehensive school website with a secure digital portal for admissions, online registration, communication, and electronic payment systems.
  • Introduce and expand extracurricular programmes and camps in Artificial Intelligence, Information Technology, and Robotics.
  • Support a formal School Alumni Network to reconnect former students, strengthen mentorship, and support institutional development.
  • Pursue corporate sponsorship partnerships to support school initiatives, student programmes, infrastructure improvements, and educational advancement.
  • Create a professional Skills Bank of qualified professionals, alumni, parents, and community members through a transparent engagement process.

My Commitment

Loyalty in service with no compromises.

  • To support student success in an evolving digital future and help SPPS continue becoming more efficient, visible, and future-ready.
