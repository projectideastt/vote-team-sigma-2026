Achieved Policy

Integrity in Financing. No Compromises.

Fundraising and Donation Policy (2026) is available now as part of the Team Sigma policy release.

Why this matters

The policy establishes a clear framework for fundraising and donations, keeping funds linked to approved student-focused projects and protecting the school from financial, legal and reputational risk.

Key safeguards

  • Transparency in fund usage and accountability through reporting.
  • Designated project use for fundraising proceeds.
  • Separate fundraising account and documented financial reconciliation.
  • Board approval and AML/CFT screening for high-value donations.
  • Conflict-of-interest disclosure and the right to reject unsuitable donations.

Community benefit

Fundraisers are connected to visible student-focused outcomes such as library development, technology upgrades, sports development, arts, enrichment activities, school clubs, gardens and welfare support.
