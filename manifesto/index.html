Our Direction

Team Sigma is focused on strengthening SPPS through accountable leadership, clearer systems, transparent governance and student-centred progress.

Priority Areas

  • Strengthened governance and Board continuity.
  • Transparent financial stewardship and policy-based decision-making.
  • Modernised administrative systems and digital communication.
  • Safe, inclusive and student-centred school operations.
  • Expanded partnerships, sponsorships and long-term community support.
